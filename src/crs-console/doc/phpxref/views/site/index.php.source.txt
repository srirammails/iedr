Registrars are expected to assume full responsibility and accountability for domain renewal/non-renewal, payments, and MSD suspensions and reactivations.
<br>
IEDR staff will remain available for support, and for query resolution, but will no longer actively intervene in the automated procedures. In particular,
<br>
<br>
<ul>
	<li>Un-processed domains in 'Renew and Pay' will be moved to the MSD list during the monthly cleardown process. This takes place on the night of the last day of each month</li>
	<li>'Offline payments' must be received by the last working day of the month, otherwise the monthly cleardown will move the related domains to the MSD process</li>
	<li>A credit card will be required to reactivate domains in the MSD process. Offline payments such as cheques or CT's will not be processed, and will be returned.</li>
	<li>Manual IEDR credit notes will be rare, but when required, will be issued with a cheque refund attached. Registrars should not deduct manual credit notes from off-line payments.</li>
</ul>
As we continue to automate the domain management processes, your continued feedback is very welcome.  Please e-mail <a href="mailto:console@iedr.ie?Subject=Feedback">console@iedr.ie</a> with your comments or suggestions.
<br>
Please visit the IEDR Registrar Discussion Forum (RDF) for general discussions, industry matters, general registration service matters and technical discussions.
